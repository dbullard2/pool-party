About Pool Party

In 1991, Pool & Party, two enterprising college kids, started Pool Party. Their goal was to grow a business to help people find the best swimwear and pool accessories out there (and maybe make enough cash to host some pool parties of their own!). Pool Party is now known around the world for their stores that pop up in the summer time.
